How to Use

Once you sign up, a free diagnosis right will be enabled for your account.

Click on the Upload Image button to diagnose your first image for free.

Choose the chest X-Ray image from your computer and click on the Submit button.

Your image will be uploaded, our Deep Learning Model will get configured and your image will be predicted.

The whole process will take about 20-30 seconds to complete.

You will be redirected to the page where you will see the diagnosis results and likelihood values i.e. the probability of having the lung cancer.

You can go to the Upload Image page to upload and diagnose new images.
